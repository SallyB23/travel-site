Travels Around the World

Follow your dreams...

Hello

I'm Sally and in 2019 I decided to fulfil my dream. I quit my job and embarked on a trip around the globe!


Places

Beaches

I have been lucky enough to visit some incredible beaches, from watching fireworks on the beach at Port Douglas in Australia to celebrating Easter under the watchful gaze of the Moai on Anakena beach on Easter Island.

Cities

Landing in the sprawling metropolis of Lima and subsequently travelling to the small mountain city of Cusco at the beginning of my journey set the scene for the many different cities I would see throughout my trip.

Woodland

Out of all the places I have visted, many of my favourites have been green, such as the cloud forest on the Inca Trail on the way to Machu Picchu. Even when in a bustling city like Sydney, it is not long before I seek out some vegitation, looking for the nearest gardens or woods to pass some time.
